Since I was very young I have explored the unknown within computer science. I joined a computer science club at the age of 7 and I soon started coding in BASIC, before I advanced to Pascal language.

I love web development because I can work with both designs and complex algorithms - it never stops changing.
I am a hard-working individual who is always looking for new and exciting opportunities within web development.

I have always been organised, detail oriented, independent and motivated so I believe that the career I've chosen is a great fit for me. I do enjoy working in a team or on my own, I am never afraid of introducing new approaches to finish a specific task. My First Website

This is the first website that I ever fully developed and finished. Previously I just had experience with web development and I thought I should finally develop a website for myself. I tried to keep website rather simple as this was my first and at that time too many features could ruin the website or make it unstable.

This website didn't have any animations or interaction but as I wanted to create somewhat professional work environment I managed to build my own gulp.js workflow. It was quite a challenge but I managed to implement browser-sync, and even started to use SASS preprocessor right away, gulp.js also watched any changes in SASS and processed them immediately. For a second I even thought of using Jade but I felt that I should not touch that for couple more websites. Sadly I didn't work with GitHub at that point and I only created a GitHub repo after the website was finished.
